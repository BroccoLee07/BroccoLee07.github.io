Floramis is an innovative app designed to bring virtual companionship to your real-life plants. As a developer and music composer for Floramis, I am involved in both frontend and backend development, ensuring smooth user experiences and robust functionality. Additionally, I contribute to the app's ambiance by composing original music that enhances the overall user experience.
