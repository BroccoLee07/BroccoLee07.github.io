About Me

Hey there! I'm Marla Nicole Seras, a Unity Software Engineer and Music Composer experienced in C# and Python. I thrive on bringing ideas and technical specifications to life through code, while also adding an extra layer of magic to the gaming experience with my knack for composing captivating melodies.


Currently, I'm having a blast working at Floramis, where I infuse creativity into my role by contributing to development and crafting immersive background music and sound effects. I also have previous experience working on projects outside the gaming industry.


I'm eagerly exploring new opportunities in Game Development, Sound Design, or Music Composition. Creating (and playing) games and writing music has always been my passion, and I would love to continue pursuing it. Don't hesitate to reach out to me through any platform. Let's collaborate on something exciting!
